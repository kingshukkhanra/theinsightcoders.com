TCS NQT

The #FutureForwardCareers National Qualifier Test (NQT) is designed with the objective to select the best talent in the country from the 2019-20 batch to fuel the growth plans of Tata Consultancy Services through its Business 4.0 Strategy.

If you are an exceptional performer in NQT, you will be a part of one of TCS’ fastest growing units - Cognitive Business Operations (CBO), Banking and Financial Services and Insurance (BFSI), and Life Sciences in various enriching roles that will amplify your career growth. Give your career a jump-start by working with Tata Consultancy Services – a leading global IT services, consulting and business solutions organization.

TCS National Qualifier Test (TNQT) 2020 is a PAN India Drive beginning with an Online Test followed by a Personal Interview which will have three rounds - Technical, Managerial & HR.

Post the assessment, interview date and location will be communicated to candidates individually.

ASSESSMENT DETAILS
Sections No. of Questions Time in Minutes
English 15 10
Quantitative Aptitude 15 30
Programming Logic 10 20
Coding 1 Problem Statement 30
Total 41 90
ASSESSMENT ELIGIBILITY
  • Qualification: Full Time Graduates from B.E / B.Tech / M.E / M.Tech / M.Sc / MCA - 2020 YOP.
  • Eligible Streams from UG/PG: All Engineering Streams.
  • Eligible Streams from M.Sc: Computer Science & Information Technology and related streams
  • % Criteria: 60% throughout Academics in X/XII/UG/Diploma/PG
  • Backlog Criteria: 1 Active Backlog at the time of appearing for the Exam
  • Gap Criteria: Overall Gap in Academic Career not to exceed 2 years
