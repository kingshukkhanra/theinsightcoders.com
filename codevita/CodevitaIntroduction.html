TCS CodeVita 2020 - Eligibility, Registration, Exam Date & Details


ELIGIBILITY

Students from institutes across India who are completing their academic course in 2021, 2022, 2023 and 2024 alone are eligible for this contest.
Registrations are invited from students in undergraduate (UG)/ postgraduate (PG) programs related to engineering/science background with any specialization.
Any Branch/Batch Student Eligibile for CodeVita.

REGISTRATION PROCESS

Step1:
To register for the contest, one has to log on to Campus Commune portal. Click here to log on to the portal now or directly visit this page - https://campuscommune.tcs.com/en-in/intro/contests/codevita-season-9.

Step2:
Click on Register option. Students who have already registered on campus commune/TCS NextStep portal can directly register for the CodeVita by entering their reference ID & password.
For new users, you will have to sign-up on campus commune and NextStep portal and then proceed to register for CodeVita.

Step3:
Once you log in, you will see the following page. Now click on Register option again.

Step4:
You will successfully register for TCS CodeVita Season 9

ROUNDS

There are three rounds in TCS codevita contest. They are

  • Pre – Qualifier Zonal round
  • Qualifier round
  • Grand Finale

IMPORTANT DATES

  • TCS Codevita Registration Starting Date: 17th March 2020
  • TCS Codevita Registration Ending Date: 5th July 2020
  • TCS Codevita Pre Qualifier Zonal Round Starting Date: 10th July 2020
  • TCS Codevita Pre Qualifier Zonal Round End Date: 25th July 2020
  • TCS Codevita Qualifier Zonal Round Starting Date: 7th November 2020
  • TCS Codevita Qualifier Zonal Round End Date: 8th November 2020
  • TCS Codevita Grand Finale Starting Date: 17th February 2020
  • TCS Codevita Grand Finale Round End Date: 17th February 2020

EXAMS PATTERN

TCS Codevita mock test exam pattern are into three rounds.

1) The Pre- Qualifier Round:
In this round, there will be a 24 hours contest. Each participant is provided with 6 hours of time to solve the questions. Pre-Qualifier round is conducted in India as well as the rest of the world separately. In this season there will be two zonal rounds in India for Pre-Qualifier. A participant will be tagged to one of the two zonal rounds and you can choose any of the 6 hours to start your challenge. TCS codevita questions are from C, C++, C#, Java, PHP, Perl, Python, Ruby

2) The Qualifier Round:
The Qualifier Round (Round 2) of TCS Codevita would happen after the completion of all the Pre-Qualifier rounds across the world. Top performers from the zonal rounds globally will move into this round. Each participant will be provided with 6 hours of time to solve & complete this round.

3) Grand Finale:
The final round of TCS Codevita will be held in one of the TCS office in India. Top 3 contestants will be declared as winners of the contest. The grand finale of TCS Codevita is tentatively scheduled to be conducted in the last week of February 2021.
