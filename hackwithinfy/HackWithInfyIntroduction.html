HackWithInfy

#HackWithInfy is a contest for all engineering students across India who are graduating in the year 2021 to inculcate the culture of rapid problem-solving and innovative thinking from early on. It provides the perfect stepping stone for students to explore their passion for programming, and an opportunity to compete and earn a chance to work with Infosys.

If you are excellent at coding, and you envision a future that is equipped with better promises and freedom to solve everyday problems with the help of technology, register for #HackWithInfy and show the world your mettle.

#HackWithInfy comprises three rounds with Round 1 and Round 2 as online tests and a Grand Finale with a 24-hour hackathon at Infosys Pune. Read the terms and conditions below for more details. Come, compete with fellow programmers who share the same passion for programming, technology, and innovation.

PRIZES

ELIGIBILITY
  • B.E./B.Tech/M.E./M.Tech students graduating in the year 2022.

3 rounds are conducted for #HackWithInfy

  • Round1: An individual online challenge
  • Round2: An individual online challenge
  • Round3: Final round that will conducted in a grand finale at our sprawling Infosys Pune campus
