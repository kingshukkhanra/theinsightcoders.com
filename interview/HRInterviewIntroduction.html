HR Interview Questions

Ever faced an HR interview before? In case you havenâ€™t, I am sure that you might face one soon enough.

Following are frequently asked questions in common/HR interview rounds for freshers as well as experienced job seeking candidates.

1. How are you today?
2. Tell me something about yourself
3. What are your key strengths?
4. What are your weaknesses?
5. Why should I hire you?
6. Why do you want to work at our company?
7. What is the difference between confidence and over confidence?
8. What is the difference between hard work and smart work?
9. Can you work under pressure?
10. What is your greatest fear?
11. Do you have any serious medical issues?
12. What do your friends/co-workers say about you?
13. What did you do in the last year to improve your knowledge?
14. What is more important to you: the money or the work?
15. What do you know about this organization?
16. What are your salary expectations?
17. Assume you are hired, then how long would you expect to work for us?
18. How would you rate yourself on a scale of 1 to 10?
19. What are your achievements in life?
20. What is your objective in life?
21. What are your hobbies?
22. Explain, how would you be an asset to this organization?
23. Would you lie for the company?
24. What does success mean to you?
25 Describe yourself in one word?
26 Just imagine that you have enough money to retire right now. Would you?
27. Don't you think that you are overqualified for this position?
28. Do you have any blind spot?
29. How do you handle stress, pressure, and anxiety?
30. Explain the difference between group and team. Are you a team player?
31. Where do you see yourself 3 years from now? or Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
32. How quickly do you adapt to new technology?
33. What motivates you?
34. What has been your greatest failure?
35. Do you have any questions?
